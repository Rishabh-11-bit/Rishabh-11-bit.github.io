FLAGSHIP

Multi-Agent Financial Research Assistant

An agentic RAG system that answers complex questions over SEC filings through four collaborating agents — a planner that decomposes the query, a retriever over a vector store, an analyst that synthesizes and cites, and a verifier that validates every claim against source documents and triggers an automatic re-draft on ungrounded output. Built with a full evaluation harness so reliability is measured, not assumed.
